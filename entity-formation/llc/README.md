# ORGANVM LLC Formation

**Status:** NOT STARTED
**Timeline:** 1-5 business days from start to bank account
**Cost:** $50-500 (home state)
**IRF:** IRF-INST-004

## Steps

- [ ] **Decide state** (home state recommended over Delaware for single-member LLC)
- [ ] **File Articles of Organization** online with Secretary of State
- [ ] **Draft Operating Agreement** (single-member, include mission statement)
- [ ] **Apply for EIN** at irs.gov (free, instant, 10 minutes)
- [ ] **Open bank account** (Mercury, Relay, or Novo — online, 1-3 days)

## PBC Conversion (Phase 2, year 1-3)

Cannot convert LLC to PBC directly. Must:
1. Statutory conversion from LLC to corporation
2. Amend articles to add public benefit purpose
3. EIN stays the same through conversion
