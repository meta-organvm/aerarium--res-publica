## Tracks Available

3. **Technical Writer** — documentation focus, up to 6 positions (3+ years experience)

## Application Form Fields
1. **Applicant info** — name/handle, employment vs freelance selection
2. **Cover letter/motivation** — 1-2 pages PDF, background in open source
3. **CV** (employment track only) — 2 pages PDF
4. **Project descriptions** — up to 3 repo URLs, 1-sentence each
5. **Maintainer role description** — 300 words, why projects are critical
6. **Dependent projects** — 300 words, who uses your technology
7. **Target audiences** — 300 words, how paid support benefits users
8. **Funding history** — 300 words, how work has been funded to date
9. **Challenges** — 300 words, what you struggle with maintaining
10. **Proposed activities** — 400-900 words (freelance) / ~900 words (employment)
11. **Hourly rate** (freelance only) — EUR, fully loaded
12. **Links** — GitHub, personal site, portfolio

## Evaluation Criteria
1. **Prevalence** — widely used across the ecosystem
2. **Relevance** — significant for important societal sectors
3. **Vulnerability** — under-funded, at risk without support
4. **Expertise** — demonstrable knowledge, community recognition
5. **Public interest** — broad or critical societal impact
