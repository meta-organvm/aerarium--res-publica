# Aspiration Tech Fiscal Sponsorship — Research Update (2026-03-30)

## Source
Perplexity Pro deep research query, 2026-03-30

## Key Findings

### Application Status
- Aspiration Tech appears to still be accepting fiscal sponsorship applications
- As of August 2024, they managed ~20 projects, adding 6-10 annually
- There was a queue of 7 projects awaiting onboarding as of 2024
- Contact: fiscalservices@aspirationtech.org for current status

### Fee Structure
- **Sliding scale** based on project resources, scope, and types of funding
- ~10% sponsorship fee was documented in 2024
- The $150 application fee could NOT be confirmed in public sources
- Fees are negotiated individually — no standard published rate
- **Action: Contact directly to verify current fees before applying**

### Application Requirements
- Mission alignment with technology, data, digital rights focus
- Open and equitable approaches to community governance and IP
- Concrete plans with defined programs and target communities
- Evidence of initial funding or funding pledges
- Organizational leadership and structure
- Fundraising plan and financial base

### What They Sponsor
- Technology, data, and digital rights projects
- Free and open source projects
- Civil society technology initiatives
- Human rights technology projects
- AI policy research (they sponsor AI Now Institute)
- International tech cooperation projects (Kaiji)
- Independent technology research (Coalition for Independent Tech Research)

### Services Provided
- HR and payroll administration
- Benefits administration (health insurance, retirement)
- Grants and donations management
- Contracts management
- Monthly financial reporting
- Dedicated bank accounts and credit cards
- Strategic mentoring (no additional cost)
- Organizational development support
- Spin-out planning for eventual independence

### Timeline
- TSNE (comparable sponsor): 9 weeks minimum, typically 3-6 months
- Mission Edge (comparable): ~4 weeks onboarding
- Aspiration: Expect queue delays — 7 projects were waiting as of 2024
- **Recommendation: Initiate contact immediately if applying**

### Recent Changes
- Propel Nonprofits sunset their fiscal sponsorship program (March 2026)
- Fiscal sponsorship landscape is consolidating
- Aspiration maintains selective, quality-focused approach

tooling, and community coordination aspects. Aspiration explicitly supports "open and equitable
